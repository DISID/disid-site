GIS Developer

At DISID we are experiencing significant growth in IT service development. This is an excellent opportunity for an experienced GIS developer to join us to become part of the team of Java web application development technician with GEO component. As a GIS developer, you will work closely with technical staff, the business department, and IT management teams.

What we look for?

The GIS Developer must have skills and experience in the following areas:

  • 3 years of experience in software development.
  • High level in Java, Javascript and Spring development.
  • Experience with large Javascript applications.
  • Strong knowledge and interest in aspects of Cartography and Geomatics.
  • Fluency in visualizing web mapping data.
  • Good practice knowledge in software development, including optimization, documentation and testing.
  • Solid knowledge of spatial databases, especially Postgis and Oracle Spatial.
  • Handling software repositories and SVN and GIT version controls.
  • Good communication and organizational skills.

Additionally, we will value working experience within a similar organization.

What we offer?

  • Salary of € 22K/year gross
  • Initially, a one-year temporary contract.
  • Solid and highly qualified professional team.
  • Flexibility of schedules.
  • Possibility of teleworking.
  • Intensive day in summer and Friday afternoon free all year.
  • A spacious and bright office, good coffee and table football.

At DISID we believe that professional development is improved with the help of a good team. Even if you think that you do not fit the profile, do not hesitate to apply if you really believe that you are a good candidate.
